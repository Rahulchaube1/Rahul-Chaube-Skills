# Video Generation

Temporal coherence, motion dynamics, prompt timelines, and framing.

## Core Guidelines

- **Motion Prompts**: Specify camera panning, speed, and framing actions explicitly.
- **Temporal Coherence**: Keep key characters and settings consistent across frames.
- **Export Formats**: Select target frame rates and extensions correctly.
